MigraineGuard - Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

About the App

MigraineGuard is a private headache tracking application designed with maximum user privacy in mind.

Data Collection and Usage

MigraineGuard does NOT collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers.

Local Data Storage

  • All headache records are stored exclusively on your device
  • Data never leaves your iPhone/iPad
  • The app does not require an internet connection to function
  • No analytics or usage statistics are collected

Export Feature

When you use the export feature:

  • A CSV file is created locally on your device
  • You have full control over who you share this file with
  • We recommend using exports for consultations with healthcare providers

App Permissions

MigraineGuard requests minimal permissions:

  • File system access - only for saving exported data
  • No permissions for camera, microphone, contacts, or location access

Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS system-level security:

  • Data is encrypted at the operating system level
  • Only the MigraineGuard app has access to its data
  • All data is automatically deleted when the app is removed

Third-Party Services

MigraineGuard does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising platforms. Your data stays completely private and local.

Children's Privacy

MigraineGuard does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, this app is safe for users of all ages.

Policy Updates

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page. We recommend checking for updates periodically.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, you don't need to worry about:

  • Data deletion requests
  • Data sharing with third parties
  • Privacy breaches
  • Data portability

HIPAA Compliance

While MigraineGuard is designed for health tracking, it is not a HIPAA-covered entity. However, since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, your health information stays completely private.
