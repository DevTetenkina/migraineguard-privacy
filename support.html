Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I export my headache data?
A: Go to the Statistics tab and tap the export button. This will create a CSV file that you can share with your healthcare provider.
Q: Is my data safe and private?
A: Yes! All your data stays on your device. MigraineGuard never sends your information to external servers. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
Q: Can I backup my data?
A: Your data is automatically backed up through iCloud if you have app data backup enabled in your iOS settings. You can also export your data as a CSV file for manual backup.
Q: The app crashed or isn't working properly
A: Try restarting the app first. If the problem persists, please restart your device. If you're still experiencing issues, contact us with details about what happened.
Q: Can I use MigraineGuard on multiple devices?
A: You can install MigraineGuard on all your iOS devices, but data doesn't automatically sync between devices. Each device maintains its own local database.
Q: How do I delete all my data?
A: Simply delete the MigraineGuard app from your device. This will permanently remove all stored headache data.
